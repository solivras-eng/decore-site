The venue

A space with a unique atmosphere

In Bagnolet, 50 metres from Paris (Porte de Montreuil, metro line 9), Le Décor à l'Envers is a lived-in loft curated by Agnès, a visual artist: natural light, generous volumes, finds from around the world, and a soul visitors feel from the moment they step inside.

The venue hosts up to about 70 guests in event configuration, with flexibility across the living room, garden, courtyard and professional kitchen — plus a 70 m² annex (La Jolie Maison) as an add-on.
