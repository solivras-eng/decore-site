Privacy policy

Last updated: May 2026. This policy describes how Le Décor à l'Envers processes personal data collected through the website decoralenvers.fr, in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and applicable French law.

1. Data controller

The controller is Le Décor à l'Envers, event and reception venue — 93170 Bagnolet, France.

Privacy contact: contact@agnesbouche.com

Add if required: full legal name, legal form, SIREN/SIRET and registered office address.

2. Data collected and purposes

Contact / quote request form: name, first name, email address, phone where provided, event dates or type, free-text message. Purposes: respond to your request, prepare a quote, arrange a visit or callback, manage the commercial relationship prior to any contract.

Browsing: technical data exchanged with the server (IP address, timestamp, browser type) for security, correct operation of the site and minimal technical logging.

Legal bases: pre-contractual measures and legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(b) and (f) GDPR) for form requests; legitimate interest for security and service stability.

3. Recipients and processors

Form data is sent to our provider Formspree (message routing and hosting — company based in the United States). Standard contractual clauses or other transfer safeguards may apply; see formspree.io.

Site hosting (HTML, images) is provided by Vercel Inc. (United States); technical logs may be kept under their policy.

We do not sell your data to third parties for marketing.

4. Retention

Messages and contact details relating to a commercial request are kept for as long as needed to handle the request, then for any statutory limitation period applicable to commercial matters or for the duration set out in any signed contract (adjust according to your internal policy and legal advice).

Hosting logs are retained according to the host’s practices.

5. Cookies and similar technologies

This site loads fonts via Google Fonts; Google may receive technical data (IP address, user agent). You can limit tracking through your browser settings. If your legal review requires it, add a consent banner or self-host fonts.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect data against unauthorised access, loss or alteration (HTTPS, reputable providers). No system is perfectly secure.

7. Your rights

Where GDPR applies you have the right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection and data portability in the situations provided by law.

To exercise your rights: contact@agnesbouche.com. You may lodge a complaint with the CNIL (French supervisory authority).

8. Children

The site is not directed at collecting data from children under 16 without appropriate parental consent where the law requires it.
