The Loft

Our story

A loft born from a vision

Le Décor à l'Envers was founded on the belief that an exceptional event deserves a setting that feels right. Founder Agnès imagined this loft as a living space — neither too rigid nor too temporary — where every detail invites creativity and celebration.

In Bagnolet, Porte de Montreuil (metro line 9), the venue combines a ~300 m² loft opening onto the garden with a 70 m² annexe (La Jolie Maison) in a leafy courtyard — enclosed grounds, greenery, natural light and flexible décor designed for productions and events.

The space works like a creative stage: shoots, seminars, weddings, filming, cocktails or workshops — layouts and capacity on quotation, with a warm welcome from Agnès.
